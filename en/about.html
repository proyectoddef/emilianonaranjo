About me

I am a university professor specialized in Physical Education, with postgraduate degrees in Education and Educational Management. My work focuses on inclusive education and human rights, particularly from an anti-ableist perspective.

I have developed experience in teaching, research, academic management, and teacher training, accompanying institutional processes aimed at ensuring accessibility, participation, and social justice.

  • PhD candidate in Education (UNTREF/UNSAM/UNLa).
  • Lecturer at the National University of Hurlingham.
  • Representative before the UN Committee on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities.
  • Recognized by UNESCO as a defender of education and inclusion.
